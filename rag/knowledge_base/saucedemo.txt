Sauce Demo (E-commerce demo):

1) Open https://www.saucedemo.com/
2) Enter username "standard_user" and password "secret_sauce".
3) Click the "Login" button.
4) On the products page, find an item card (e.g., "Sauce Labs Backpack").
5) Click "Add to cart" under that item.
6) Verify that the "Remove" button or cart icon shows an updated count.
Selectors:
- Username input: #user-name
- Password input: #password
- Login button: #login-button
- Add to cart button: button:has-text("Add to cart")
- Remove button: button:has-text("REMOVE")
- Cart icon: a.shopping_cart_link
